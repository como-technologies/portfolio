# Services

Consulting and enablement services that wrap the portfolio into engagements. Services are what turn the TAPS tools into outcomes — the thread that connects Assess to Measure.

- **Assessment engagements** — Facilitated discovery using the assessments app. Interviews, artifact review, and AI-assisted synthesis into a structured maturity model. Output: an actionable assessment, not a deck.
- **Playbook authoring** — Co-creation of an opinionated playbook tailored to your context, using palette-playbook as a reference format and adroit as the authoring tool. Output: a living playbook your teams can extend.
- **Adoption enablement** — Pairing, workshops, and incremental rollout that gets the playbook into daily practice, increasingly accelerated by [conduit](../tools/) *(in development)* — the agentic harness that works decisions as PRs in your own forge while humans keep the gates. This is where most modernization programs stall; it's where Como spends the most effort.
- **Measurement setup** — Standing up pulse and tuesday so adoption is observed, not assumed. Includes baseline capture, dashboarding, and the cadence that feeds back into the next assessment.

Every engagement runs the full loop by default, but each service is available on its own for clients who already have their own shape.
